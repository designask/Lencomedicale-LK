How It Works

A Simple, Transparent Process

Our recruitment matching process is designed to be straightforward, professional, and respectful of your privacy at every stage.

For Doctors

Your Journey in 4 Simple Steps

From first submission to medical centre introduction — here's how we connect you with opportunities.

1

Doctor Submits CV

Complete our secure online form with your professional details, qualifications, and upload your CV.

  • Personal and contact details
  • Medical qualifications and specialty
  • Registration status and experience
  • CV/resume upload (PDF or Word)
  • Consent acknowledgements

Secure Submission

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Only authorised team members can access your information.

Thorough Review

Every profile is reviewed by our experienced team within 5 business days of submission.

2

We Review Experience & Documents

Our team carefully assesses your qualifications, experience, registration status, and career preferences.

  • Qualification verification
  • Experience and specialty assessment
  • Registration pathway understanding
  • Availability and preference matching
3

We Identify Suitable Opportunities

Based on your profile, we identify medical centres within our network that match your qualifications, specialty, and availability.

  • Match specialty with centre requirements
  • Consider experience level and role type
  • Align availability and start date preferences
  • Assess registration pathway compatibility

Intelligent Matching

We don't just send CVs randomly — we carefully match profiles with relevant opportunities.

Privacy First

Your CV is only shared with medical centres after your explicit consent. You remain in control.

4

Candidate Introduction with Consent

We introduce your profile to interested medical centres only after obtaining your explicit consent, ensuring complete privacy throughout.

  • We contact you before sharing any information
  • You decide which introductions to proceed with
  • Professional introduction managed by our team
  • Ongoing support throughout the process

FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a fee for doctors to submit their CV?

No. Submitting your CV and using our matching service is completely free for doctors.

Will my CV be listed publicly?

Never. Your profile is stored securely and only shared with specific medical centres after you give your consent.

Do you guarantee a job placement?

No. We help connect and introduce profiles — final hiring decisions are made by medical centres based on their own requirements, and opportunities depend on suitability, registration, and availability.

Can I request my data be deleted?

Yes. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. We will remove your information from our systems promptly.

Do I need to be in Australia already?

No. We accept submissions from doctors currently overseas, recently arrived in Australia, or already in Australia looking for new opportunities.
