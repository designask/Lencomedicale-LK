Our Story

Why We Exist

Lencomedicale LK was created to make the doctor recruitment connection process simpler, more professional, and more transparent.

We recognised that many talented, qualified doctors trained overseas face challenges when trying to connect with Australian healthcare opportunities. At the same time, medical centres across Australia often struggle to find suitable candidates to fill their staffing needs.

Our mission is to bridge that gap — by collecting CVs from international doctors, reviewing their profiles professionally, and introducing suitable candidates to trusted Australian medical centres.

We support overseas-qualified doctors by providing a clear, confidential pathway to explore opportunities, while helping medical centres access a curated pool of qualified international professionals.

Connecting Talent with Opportunity

What We Do

Our Role in the Recruitment Process

We are clear about what we do and what we don't do.

What We Do

  • Collect and securely store doctor CVs
  • Review professional profiles and qualifications
  • Help connect suitable profiles with medical centres
  • Introduce suitable profiles with candidate consent
  • Support the recruitment connection process
  • Maintain confidentiality and data privacy

What We Don't Do

  • Guarantee job placements or employment
  • Guarantee medical registration or visa approval
  • Provide migration or legal advice
  • Act as a medical registration authority
  • List doctor profiles publicly
  • Sell candidate data to third parties
Our Values

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every aspect of our work

Privacy & Confidentiality

We take data protection seriously. Your information is stored securely and never shared without your explicit consent.

Honesty & Transparency

We are upfront about what we do, how we work, and what candidates can expect. No false promises, no hidden processes.

Respect & Support

We treat every doctor with respect, understanding the challenges of relocating and navigating a new healthcare system.

Professionalism

From CV handling to introductions, everything is managed with the highest level of professionalism.

Integrity

We only introduce candidates to medical centres when there's a genuine fit. Quality over quantity, always.

Inclusivity

We welcome doctors from all backgrounds and countries, supporting diversity in Australian healthcare.

Our Commitment

An Honest Approach

We believe in being transparent about what we can and cannot do. Here's our honest commitment to you:

  • We help connect — we don't guarantee outcomes
  • We introduce suitable profiles — final decisions rest with medical centres
  • We support the recruitment connection process — we don't replace professional advisors
  • Opportunities depend on suitability, registration, employer requirements, and availability
  • We will always communicate clearly and professionally with every candidate
