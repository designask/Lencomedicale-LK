Eligibility

Who Can Register?

We work with qualified medical specialists from Sri Lanka who are looking to practise in Australia. To be eligible, you should have:

  • MBBS or equivalent medical degree
  • Specialist qualification or training in progress
  • Minimum 2 years post-qualification experience
  • Valid IELTS/OET score (or willingness to sit)
  • Clean medical registration in Sri Lanka
