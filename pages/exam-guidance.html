Structured guidance and resources for AMC, specialty college exams, and AHPRA registration. Our mentors have been through the process and know what it takes to succeed.

Exams We Cover

Comprehensive Exam Support

From initial AMC exams to specialty fellowship assessments

AMC MCQ (CAT)

The Australian Medical Council Computer Adaptive Test is the first step. We provide study plans, question banks, and mentoring to help you pass on your first attempt.

AMC Clinical

The clinical examination tests practical skills. Our guidance includes clinical scenarios, practice sessions with experienced mentors, and mock exams.

RACP Examinations

For physicians — guidance on Written and Clinical examinations for the Royal Australasian College of Physicians fellowship.

RACS Examinations

For surgeons — preparation support for the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons fellowship examinations including generic and specialty-specific components.

RANZCOG Examinations

For obstetricians and gynaecologists — guidance on DRANZCOG and FRANZCOG examination pathways.

RANZCP Examinations

For psychiatrists — support for the Royal Australian and New Zealand College of Psychiatrists fellowship examinations.

RANZCO Examinations

For ophthalmologists — preparation guidance for the Royal Australian and New Zealand College of Ophthalmologists examinations.

OET / IELTS

English language proficiency is essential. We help you prepare for OET (preferred) or IELTS Academic to meet registration requirements.
