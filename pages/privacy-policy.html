Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Lencomedicale LK ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

By using our website or submitting your information through our forms, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

From Doctors:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Current country and location status
  • Medical qualifications, specialty, and experience
  • Registration status and visa information
  • CV/resume documents, cover letters, certificates
  • LinkedIn profile URL (if provided)
  • Availability and role preferences

From Medical Centres:

  • Centre name, contact person, email, phone
  • Type of centre and staffing requirements
  • Role type and urgency information

From All Visitors:

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message)
  • Website usage data (via analytics, if enabled)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To review and assess doctor profiles for recruitment matching
  • To match suitable candidates with medical centre opportunities
  • To communicate with you about potential opportunities
  • To introduce your profile to medical centres (with your consent)
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our services and website functionality

4. How We Share Your Information

Your information may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • With medical centres: Only after your explicit consent, as part of a recruitment introduction
  • With service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our services (e.g., email providers, hosting), bound by confidentiality obligations
  • As required by law: If legally compelled by Australian or applicable law

We will never: Sell your data to third parties, list your profile publicly, or share your information without consent for recruitment purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely with access restricted to authorised personnel only
  • CVs and documents are not publicly accessible
  • We use encryption and secure protocols for data transmission
  • Regular security reviews are conducted to protect your information
  • Admin access is password-protected and restricted

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or until you request deletion. Typically:

  • Active candidate profiles: Retained for up to 24 months from last interaction
  • Medical centre enquiries: Retained for up to 12 months
  • Contact form messages: Retained for up to 6 months
  • After retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymised

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@lencomedicale.lk. We will respond within 14 business days.

8. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand website usage patterns. These do not collect personally identifiable information from forms. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us:
