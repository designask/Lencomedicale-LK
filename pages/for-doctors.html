Submit Your CV for Australian Medical Opportunities

If you are a doctor trained outside Australia and looking to explore opportunities with medical centres in Australia, submit your CV and professional details. Our team will review your profile and contact you if suitable opportunities are available.

Who Can Apply

We Work With Doctors Across All Stages

Whether you're planning to move, recently arrived, or already practising — we welcome your application.

General Practitioners

GPs with overseas qualifications looking for clinic-based roles across metropolitan and regional Australia.

Specialists

Specialist doctors in areas such as internal medicine, surgery, psychiatry, paediatrics, and more.

Resident Medical Officers

Junior doctors and RMOs seeking hospital-based positions and training pathway opportunities.

Emergency Doctors

Emergency medicine practitioners interested in ED roles across Australian hospitals.

Locum or Permanent

Whether you're looking for short-term locum positions or long-term permanent roles, we can assist.

Planning to Move

Doctors still overseas who are planning to relocate to Australia and want to explore opportunities early.

After You Submit

What Happens Next

A clear, transparent process from submission to potential opportunity

1

CV Received

We acknowledge receipt of your submission and securely store your documents.

2

Profile Review

Our team reviews your qualifications, registration status, and preferences within 5 business days.

3

Matching

If suitable opportunities exist, we identify medical centres that match your profile.

4

Introduction

With your consent, we introduce your profile to interested medical centres for further discussion.

Be Prepared

What You'll Need to Submit

  • Updated CV/Resume — PDF or Word format preferred
  • Professional details — qualifications, specialty, experience
  • Registration status — AHPRA, AMC pathway progress, or plans
  • Availability — when you can start or are planning to arrive
  • Role preference — permanent, locum, full-time, part-time

Important Disclaimer

We are not a migration agent, legal advisor, or medical registration authority. We help with CV collection, candidate introduction, and recruitment connection only. Doctors must check their own eligibility, registration pathway, visa status, and professional requirements with the relevant Australian authorities including AHPRA, the Medical Board of Australia, and the Department of Home Affairs.
